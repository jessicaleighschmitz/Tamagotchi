Tamagotchi

To play: try to keep your Tamagotchi happy. Use the keyboard key to feed her, clean her room, show her affection, and help her get some sleep!

Key

  • Spacebar = medium feed
  • a = small feed
  • s = large feed
  • d = gross feed
  • z = clean
  • x = love
  • c = nap
  • v = long nap
